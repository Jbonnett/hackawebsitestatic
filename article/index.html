Technical Career Knowledge Roadmap December 6, 2023

To those who want to learn Computer Security: Make sure you don’t focus solely on JUST what is considered cybersecurty and hacking, and instead learn a fair bit about how computers and networks work as well. Here is a rough list: A bit of programing. At least enough to be able to write a script to process text. A bit of digital electronics. At least enough to know what a and gate and a xor is.

*This website does NOT condown illegal actions, or even legal but immoral ones. Knowledge is power, and power is responsibility. Don't be a dumb ass.
